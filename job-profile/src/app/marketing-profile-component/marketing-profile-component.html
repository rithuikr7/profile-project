About Lynqa

Lynqa Strategic Finance is a boutique capital strategy partner, not a distributor, not an advisor. We work behind the spotlight, helping businesses align intent with investor expectations through strategic documentation, deal structuring, and market-savvy execution.

We don’t pitch products or chase mandates. We decode market appetite, sharpen deal narratives, and facilitate precision-led negotiations for fund-raising instruments like Commercial Papers and Non-Convertible Debentures (NCDs).

By remaining unregistered with SEBI, we focus purely on management consulting, ensuring compliance boundaries are respected, while companies benefit from sophisticated structuring and execution clarity.

Whether you're raising your first CP or optimizing recurring issuance, Lynqa steps in where noise ends and strategy begins.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you SEBI registered?

No. Lynqa Strategic Finance does not provide investment advice or distribute financial products. We do not fall under SEBI’s registration mandate.

So what exactly do you offer?

We assist companies with capital strategy, deal negotiation, documentation, and structuring - specifically around debt instruments like commercial papers and NCDs.

Do you help investors?

We do not manage investor capital or offer investment advice. Our engagement is with companies seeking to raise funds at optimal terms.

Can you draft term sheets or support documentation?

Yes, we assist in drafting term sheets, coordinating documentation, and facilitating clean execution of structured debt deals.
