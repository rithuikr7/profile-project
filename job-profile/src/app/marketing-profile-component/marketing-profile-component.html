About

Lynqa Strategic Finance operates as a strategic consulting brand that supports companies in navigating private debt markets—without offering regulated investment advice.

We are not registered under SEBI and do not distribute financial products or provide investment recommendations. Our focus lies in facilitating structured conversations between companies and prospective capital providers, primarily through commercial paper and in future, non-convertible debentures (NCDs).

By analyzing yield expectations, market behavior, and deal structures, we assist in negotiating terms that align with both issuer and investor interests—offering clarity, structure and access without overstepping regulatory lines.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you SEBI registered?

No. Lynqa Strategic Finance does not provide investment advice or distribute financial products. We do not fall under SEBI’s registration mandate.

So what exactly do you offer?

We assist companies with capital strategy, deal negotiation, documentation, and structuring—specifically around debt instruments like commercial papers and NCDs.

Do you help investors?

We do not manage investor capital or offer investment advice. Our engagement is with companies seeking to raise funds at optimal terms.

Can you draft term sheets or support documentation?

Yes, we assist in drafting term sheets, coordinating documentation, and facilitating clean execution of structured debt deals.
